# AI Governance Controls

## Risk
AI-generated compliance artifacts may not reflect reality.

## Controls

### 1. Identification
- Flag AI-generated policies, risks, and evidence

### 2. Human Validation
- Require manual review and approval

### 3. Traceability
- Ensure evidence can be traced to real activity

### 4. Restrictions
- Prohibit fully automated control evidence without validation

## Goal
Ensure AI supports compliance — not replaces accountability.
